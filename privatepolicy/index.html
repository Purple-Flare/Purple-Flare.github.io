Private Policy

One of our main priorities is the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Flare and how we use it.

Your Data at Flare

When you use Flare, you send basic information about your workspace to Flare. We use this information for basic analytics so that we may improve our services. We do not collect nor store any personal information for any members in the workspace. We also do not sell any information to any third parties whatsoever.

Link Collection

Whenever a link is scanned by our service, we store the results in order to improve our detection capabilities. However, we encrypt these links before we store them so that no one, not even us, can view them.

Security

We appreciate that you trust us with your information, and we take great precaution to keep that data safe. We are partnered with security firms to ensure that our data is secured. Remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Changes to our Public Policy

We may alter our public policy at any time. Therefore, you may periodically check our public policy for any updates. We will notify our users for any changes in public policy, and these changes are effective immediately.
